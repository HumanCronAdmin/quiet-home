Quick Reference

What do decibel levels sound like?

Under 30 dB — Whisper

Barely audible. Like a quiet library or leaves rustling. Sleep-friendly.

30-44 dB — Quiet

Noticeable but not disruptive. Like a refrigerator humming. Fine for home offices.

45-60 dB — Moderate

Clearly audible. Like a normal conversation. You'll hear it across the room.

60+ dB — Loud

Hard to talk over. Like a running shower or busy restaurant. Not apartment-friendly.
